Workflow Redesign

Augmentation is not bolting AI onto a job. It is redesigning a process around human and AI. Pick a workflow, then switch from before to after and watch where AI enters and where you stay. Part of the AI Fluency toolkit.

View

The steps AI can take are the documented, repeatable ones: intake, rule checking, drafting, logging. The human keeps the steps that carry judgment, money, and accountability. And notice which steps could move at all: only the ones already written down. A step that lives in someone's head cannot be handed to AI until it is documented. The workflow you can augment is the workflow you can explain.
