Trust Dial

How much should you let AI do? It is not one setting you flip for the whole company. It is per task, and where it can safely sit depends on the stakes and on whether you have done the work to be there. Pick a task, move the dial, and watch the safe zone change. Part of the AI Fluency toolkit.

The dial is the gate made operational. Moving up is not a question of how clever the model is, it is a question of stakes, reversibility, and readiness. Each step toward autonomy assumes the process underneath is documented and verified, monitored while it runs, and reversible when it goes wrong. That is the same rule as the rest of the suite: do not automate what you cannot explain. The safe zone is not fixed, it is earned, task by task.
