AI faithfully reproduces the corpus it retrieves from. A corpus that contradicts itself produces an assistant that contradicts itself, with full confidence, because the model cannot tell a canonical source from a stale variant when nothing in the architecture marks the difference. You cannot prompt your way out of that. The fix is not a better model, it is a corpus that has earned faithfulness: contradictions reconciled into one canonical source, the old variants superseded with their lineage preserved. That is epistemic debt, and paying it down is the work that has to come before the tooling.
