How to read this

Every green line is one capability or team. They all start as short stubs on the left. Over time some advance faster than others. The amber line is the gate: nothing branches into blue automation until the work behind it is documented and verified. Spirals mean the line is still in motion, not finished.

Human layer. The role keeps the decisions and never disappears.
Machine layer. Automations the role starts and owns, always tethered to their green trunk.
The gate. Do not automate what you cannot explain.
