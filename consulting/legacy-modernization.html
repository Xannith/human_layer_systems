Consulting — Legacy Documentation Modernization

Decades of knowledge.
None of it usable at scale.

Organizations accumulate years of policy, process, procedure, and institutional knowledge in formats that were never designed to scale, be searched, or be consumed by AI. This engagement transforms that accumulated knowledge into a structured, maintainable, and AI-ready content library.

When this engagement is the right fit

Post-merger integration

Two organizations have merged, each with its own documentation systems, terminology, and institutional knowledge. The combined system is incoherent and unmaintainable.

Platform migration

Your organization is moving to a new knowledge management platform and the legacy content cannot simply be copied over. It needs to be restructured to fit the new system and governance model.

Operational modernization

Years of operational documentation have accumulated without governance. Conflicting procedures exist side by side. Nobody knows which version is current. The system has become a liability.

AI adoption enablement

Your organization wants to deploy AI tools but the existing content is not structured to support reliable retrieval. Modernization is the prerequisite for AI readiness.

What modernization involves

Inventory and triage

Complete mapping of existing content. What exists, where it lives, how old it is, whether it is still accurate, and which of it has operational value worth preserving.

Architecture definition

Defining the target state: how content should be typed, structured, and governed in the modernized system. This becomes the specification that all subsequent migration work is measured against.

Structured migration

Systematic transformation of legacy content into the target architecture: restructuring, retyping, applying metadata, resolving conflicts, and retiring what is no longer relevant.

Governance handoff

Documentation of the new system, training for content owners, and the governance model that ensures the modernized content stays accurate and well-maintained going forward.

Deliverable

Migrated, structured content library with full metadata, taxonomy alignment, and governance documentation.

Timeline: Typically 8–16 weeks depending on content volume and legacy system complexity.
