A note on the technical side

If your organization has already deployed AI tools and they’re underperforming — wrong answers, outdated content, inconsistent results — the problem is almost never the model. It’s the content feeding it. Enterprise AI runs on your knowledge layer, and if that layer wasn’t structured for machine retrieval, no amount of model tuning will fix it.

The consulting engagements above include content architecture and governance work where needed. If you already know you have a knowledge infrastructure problem and want to go straight to that conversation, the Knowledge System Audit is the right starting point.
