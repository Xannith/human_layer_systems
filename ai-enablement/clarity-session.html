How the commitment works

That’s not arbitrary. Real habit change and genuine skill-building take time to show up in your actual work. Three months is long enough to see measurable difference in how you work. Short enough that it’s a manageable commitment to start.

Month-to-month after that

After three months, you continue as long as it’s useful. Most people do. There’s no pressure to stay if you’ve gotten what you came for — but in practice, the work keeps evolving and so do the questions.
