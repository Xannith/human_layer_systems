The Clarity Session

The entry point. Steady monthly momentum at an accessible price. Three sessions a month, open access between them, and a real working relationship focused on where you are right now.

Who this is for

An individual who wants to get genuinely good at AI — not just dabble. You have real work to do and you want someone in your corner who can help you figure out where AI fits, build the habits that stick, and troubleshoot when things don’t work the way you expected. You’re not looking for a one-time answer. You’re looking for ongoing momentum.

What each month includes

Three individual sessions per month — each one focused on your current questions, your current work, and wherever you are in your AI journey. Between sessions, you have open access via email or text. Ask questions, share something you tried, flag something that isn’t working. You get a real answer, not a form response, usually quickly or at our next session if it needs more thought.

What we work on together

We look at your specific job and your specific problems. Workflows you want to speed up. Tasks you want to hand off to AI. Prompts that aren’t working the way you thought. Automations where they make sense. Over time, the goal is for you to stop asking “can AI help with this?” and start reaching for the right tool automatically.

How the commitment works

Three-month minimum. That’s not arbitrary — real habit change and genuine skill-building take time to show up in your actual work. After three months, you can continue month to month for as long as it’s useful. Most people do.

The Spark Solo

A 90-day transformation engagement. One to two sessions per week, real deliverables every session, and a fundamentally different way of working by Day 90. Not a check-in program — a rebuild.

Who this is for

An individual who is done with surface-level AI use and ready to go deeper. You have real complexity in your work — multiple workflows, multiple problems, multiple things you want AI to handle so you can focus on what only you can do. You want a fundamentally different way of working by the end, not just better answers to the same questions.

How the 90 days work

Weeks 1–2: Audit and map. We identify your five highest-leverage AI opportunities. Weeks 3–10: Build and integrate. One system per two weeks — prompts, workflows, automations — each session produces something real you can use immediately. Weeks 11–13: Document and hand off. Everything we built gets documented. You learn to maintain it. We exit. You own it.

Session structure

One to two sessions per week, up to three contact hours total per week. 90-minute or 2-hour sessions — your choice depending on what we’re working on. Async access throughout for quick unblocks between sessions.

How it’s different from The Clarity Session

The Clarity Session is $650/month of steady, ongoing support — three monthly sessions, between-session guidance, and practical help using AI in your real work. The Spark Solo is a fixed 90-day engagement at $9,500 with defined phases, real deliverables, and a clean exit. The Clarity Session keeps you moving. The Spark Solo changes how you work.
