AI Fluency — Team Programs

Your team needs to move on AI.
The question is how fast.

These three programs cover the same ground at different speeds. A half-day workshop to get aligned, a four-week sprint to build the foundation fast, or a steady three-month build for teams that need to absorb without overwhelming. Pick the pace that fits your organization.

The Spark

A half-day workshop that gets your team aligned, fluent in the basics, and moving in the same direction. The fastest way to turn scattered AI experimentation into a shared starting point.

Who this is for

Teams of up to 15 that need a structured kickoff. Your people may already be using AI on their own, or they may be starting from zero. Either way, you need shared language, shared guardrails, and a clear next step as a group.

How it works

Pre-session survey to understand where your team is. Half-day facilitated workshop using your real work, not generic examples. Post-session survey to measure the delta. You see the before and after in the same day.

What your team leaves with

A one-page team action guide. A generic AI quick-reference card. Survey results showing measurable confidence increase. And a shared foundation to build on.

Foundation Sprint

Four weeks. Fast track. You want the foundation built now, not over months. Every week has a deliverable and by Week 4 your team has an SOP, a scorecard, and a clear picture of exactly where they stand.

Who this is for

Teams ready to move quickly. You have leadership buy-in, people who are willing to do the work, and an organization that benefits from fast results. Four weeks minimum, outcomes measured weekly.

What happens each week

Week 1: AI fluency assessment and team profile. We learn where your team actually is. Week 2: Core training sessions with homework. Real work, real application. Week 3: Your white-labeled, branded SOP draft delivered. Week 4: SOP finalized and crawl-walk-run scorecard delivered. You know exactly where you are.

How you’ll know it worked

The SOP is in use, not in a folder. Your core team can operate their AI workflows without you. Your organization knows exactly where they sit on the crawl-walk-run scale. And the decision maker is ready to talk about what comes next.

Foundation Steady

Same depth as the Sprint, spread across three months. Designed for organizations that need to build capability alongside existing work without overwhelming the team. Absorption is measured over time, not just at the end.

Who this is for

Teams that can’t pause everything for four weeks. Your people have full plates. You want the foundation built right, but at a pace that doesn’t create its own resistance. Three month minimum.

What happens each month

Month 1: Same as Sprint Weeks 1 and 2. Assessment, profile, and core training. Month 2: SOP draft delivered plus one full department deep dive. Month 3: SOP finalized, full crawl-walk-run assessment, and a habit check-in session to make sure it’s sticking.

How you’ll know it worked

Staff confidence builds gradually and visibly. Nobody felt overwhelmed. At month three, the renewal conversation or the Tier 3 conversation happens naturally — because the results earned it.
